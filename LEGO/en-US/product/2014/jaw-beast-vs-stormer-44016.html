44016 JAW Beast vs. STORMER

Prepare for action, heroes! JAW Beast, a ferocious creature with vicious claws and many eyes, has emerged from the crevice left by the earthquake and is threatening the civilians of Antropolis City. Can STORMER warn the heroes of the threat in time using his com device? Will a spear be enough to take down the mighty creature or will JAW Beast grab STORMER with its snapping mouth and drag him underground?
