44019 ROCKA Stealth Machine

Hurry, ROCKA, go into invisible mode! Power up with the Stealth Machine at LEGO® Hero Factory and venture onto the city streets. The mission is clear – save the other heroes and capture the evil jumpers using the grab claw. Jumpers are everywhere waiting to attack so keep the seat visor down and goo shooter and scanning canister ready at all times.
