44017 STORMER Freeze Machine

Get ready for battle, STORMER! In the fight to take back the city streets from giant beasts and wild little jumpers, your new Freeze Machine suit is a game-changer. Strap yourself in and support the other heroes. Fire missiles at the invaders and freeze them with the rotating ice blaster. Then capture the jumpers in the cryo chamber for analysis back at LEGO® Hero Factory.
