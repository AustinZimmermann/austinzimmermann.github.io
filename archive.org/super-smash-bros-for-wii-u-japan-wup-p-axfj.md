## Description

This item contains a complete digital preservation of the Japanese release of ***Super Smash Bros. for Wii U*** (Japanese: **大乱闘スマッシュブラザーズ for Wii U**, Hepburn: *Dairantō Sumasshu Burazāzu fō Wī Yū*). Released on December 6, 2014, in Japan, this title represents the fourth major entry in Nintendo’s premier crossover fighting franchise, developed under the direction of Masahiro Sakurai by Bandai Namco Studios and Sora Ltd.

This entry introduces 8-player local matches, HD graphics to the franchise, custom Amiibo figure configuration, and an array of new gameplay components such as Smash Tour and Special Orders.

### Technical File Contents
- **`WUP-P-AXFJ.wud`**: Full 1:1 raw unencrypted optical disc dump (23.3 GB).
- **`WUP-P-AXFJ.wux`**: Lossless compressed WUD archive optimized for emulator platforms (e.g., Cemu).
- **`/unpacked/`**: Decrypted installation folder containing raw `/code/`, `/content/`, and `/meta/` structures for direct verification.
- **`/scans/`**: High-resolution 600 DPI archival flatbed scans of the retail box sleeve, front/back insert, and the physical Wii U proprietary optical disc face.
