This is a full, 1:1 unencrypted archival backup of the 2008 biographical film Milk, directed by Gus Van Sant and starring Sean Penn. The release is preserved on a dual-layer BD-50 physical medium configuration.

The video content utilizes the native VC-1 compression codec, retaining full bitrates, high-definition grain structures, original primary uncompressed audio tracks, and full interactive pop-up menus accurate to its commercial standard release on March 10, 2009.
