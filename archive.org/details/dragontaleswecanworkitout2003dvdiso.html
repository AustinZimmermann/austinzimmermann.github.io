About

Join six-year old Emmy and her four-year old brother Max on five enchanted learning adventures in Dragon Land in this beloved animated series co-produced by the folks at Sesame Workshop and Sony Pictures Television. Kids learn important lessons about problem solving, sharing, and emotional growth.

Episodes Included:

  • Cassie, the Green-Eyed Dragon - Cassie handles jealousy when her baby brother Finn visits school.
  • So Long Solo - Zak & Weezie struggle to compromise for a big Dragon Land talent show.
  • Breaking Up is Hard to Do - Max and Ord accidentally break their craft project while fighting over it.
  • The Grudge Won’t Budge - A physical Grudge monster attaches to Zak when he refuses to forgive Weezie.
  • Remember the Pillow Fort - The gang negotiates space and sharing while building an epic blanket and pillow fort.
