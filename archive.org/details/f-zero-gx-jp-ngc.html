F-Zero GX is a high-speed futuristic racing game co-developed by Amusement Vision and Nintendo for the Nintendo GameCube console. This is the pristine, untouched Japanese release dump (GFZJ01). It runs at an intense 60 frames per second, featuring high-fidelity custom tracks, the Story Mode tracking Captain Falcon’s career, and custom vehicle customization configurations.
