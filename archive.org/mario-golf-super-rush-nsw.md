### Item Description

A complete archive of ***Mario Golf: Super Rush*** for the Nintendo Switch (NSW). Originally announced during the February 2021 Nintendo Direct, this is the ninth overall installment in the *Mario Golf* series and the first home console entry since 2003. Developed by sports-game veteran **Camelot Software Planning**, the game combines traditional mechanical precision with fast-paced, arcade-style multiplayer modes.

#### Key Features Preserved:
* **Standard Golf & Shot Gauge:** A revamped shot system departing from the classic three-click system, featuring a more dynamic power gauge that accounts for elevation, terrain friction, and wind intensity.
* **Speed Golf:** A high-octane mode where up to four players tee off simultaneously and physically sprint across the course to chase down their balls, utilizing stamina gauges, character-specific dashes, and special sabotage moves.
* **Battle Golf:** Set in a neon-lit neon arena stadium featuring nine holes simultaneously. The first player to claim three holes wins.
* **Golf Adventure (Story Mode):** A single-player role-playing campaign where players import their custom Mii™ avatar as a novice trainee at the Bonny Greens country club, earning experience points through challenges to level up specific attributes (Power, Stamina, Speed, Control, Spin).
* **Control Variety:** Native preservation supporting traditional button configurations as well as motion control emulation via a single Joy-Con™ controller.
