Synopsis

Join Leo, June, Quincy, and Annie on a grand musical mission as they use their smarts and teamwork to complete their greatest adventure yet! In this exciting DVD, the Little Einsteins travel around the world in their trusty friend, Rocket, encountering famous landmarks, solving puzzles using classical music, and learning about great works of art. This collection features the two-part special “Our Big Huge Adventure” where the team must race against the clock to retrieve a musical treasure. It’s a journey filled with art history, geography, and fun, interactive challenges designed to encourage audience participation.

Special Features

  • ★ “Little Einsteins Save the Day” Game: An interactive, remote-controlled adventure game accessible via the DVD menu.
  • ★ Exclusive Episode: Includes the bonus episode “The Birthday Balloons” (not previously aired on television).
  • ★ “Meet the Team” Featurette: Short clips introducing the children and Rocket.
  • ★ Printable Activity Guide: A PDF guide for parents with educational activities related to the DVD’s content (Requires DVD-ROM drive).
