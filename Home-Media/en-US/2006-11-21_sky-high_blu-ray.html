Sky High (2005)

Available on Blu-ray Disc: November 21, 2006

Saving The World... One Homework Assignment At A Time.

From the creators of The Princess Diaries comes Disney’s SKY HIGH. It’s high adventure at the first and only high school for kids with super-human powers. Will Stronghold is the son of the legendary Commander and Jetstream, but he has no apparent superpowers of his own. He enters high school as a “Sidekick” rather than a “Hero”. But when an evil villain threatens his family, Will must discover his true strength.
