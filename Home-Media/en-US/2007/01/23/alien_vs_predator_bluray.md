### **Product Overview**
Experience the ultimate intergalactic showdown in high definition. On **January 23, 2007**, 20th Century Fox Home Entertainment brings the crossover event of the century to the Blu-ray format. Witness the iconic Predators and the terrifying Aliens battle for supremacy on Earth, now with the pristine clarity and immersive audio that only Blu-ray can provide.

### **Special Features**
This Blu-ray release includes a selection of bonus content to take you deeper into the hunt:

* **Audio Commentaries:**
    * Commentary by Director Paul W.S. Anderson and actors Lance Henriksen and Sanaa Lathan.
    * Commentary by Visual Effects Supervisors John Bruno and Alec Gillis/Tom Woodruff Jr.
* **The Making of *AVP*:** A behind-the-scenes look at the production and creature design.
* **Search for the Predator:** An interactive trivia track.
* **Trailers:** Original theatrical trailers in high definition.

---

> **Note to Collectors:** This 2007 release features the **Theatrical Version** of the film (101 Minutes). The MPEG-2 encode and DTS-HD MA audio track provide a significant leap in quality over the previous DVD editions, making it a must-own for sci-fi enthusiasts.
