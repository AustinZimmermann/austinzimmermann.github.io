Synopsis

When a private satellite encounters an ancient pyramid buried deep beneath the ice of Antarctica, billionaire Charles Bishop Weyland (Lance Henriksen) assembles a team of scientists and explorers to investigate. What they find is far more terrifying than they ever imagined: a ritualistic hunting ground where two of the galaxy’s most lethal species have been warring for centuries. As the humans find themselves caught in the middle of the ultimate battle, they must choose a side or face extinction.
