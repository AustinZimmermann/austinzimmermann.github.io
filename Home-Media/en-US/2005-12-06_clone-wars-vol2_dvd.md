#### **Product Overview**

From director Genndy Tartakovsky, the final chapters of the *Star Wars: Clone Wars* saga are here! Witness the harrowing adventures of the Jedi Knights as they lead the Grand Army of the Republic against the droid forces of the Confederacy of Independent Systems.

This volume leads directly into the opening moments of *Star Wars*: Episode III *Revenge of the Sith*! Experience the introduction of the formidable General Grievous and the ultimate trials of Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi.
