Film Synopsis

This section provides the core narrative summary of the film as presented on the physical release, outlining the world-building, key character mutations, and the central conflict.

Witness the evolution of spectacular action and excitement in this dazzling “feast of fantasy” unlike anything you’ve ever seen before. Born into a world filled with prejudice are children who possess extraordinary and dangerous powers—the result of unique genetic mutations.

  • Cyclops unleashes bolts of energy from his eyes.
  • Storm can manipulate the weather at will.
  • Rogue absorbs the life force of anyone she touches.

But under the tutelage of Professor Xavier (Patrick Stewart), these and other outcasts learn to harness their powers for the good of mankind.

Now they must protect those who fear them as they wage a war against Magneto (Ian McKellen), who believes humans and mutants can never co-exist and has a sinister plan for the future!
