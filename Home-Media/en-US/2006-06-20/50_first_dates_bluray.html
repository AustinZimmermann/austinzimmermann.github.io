Blu-ray Presentation

Video Quality

Presented in its original 2.40:1 aspect ratio, this early Blu-ray release uses the MPEG-2 codec. While newer releases favor AVC, the colors of the Hawaiian landscape remain vibrant, with deep blues and lush greens that showcase the high-definition format's early potential.

Audio Performance

The standout feature is the Uncompressed PCM 5.1 track (48kHz/16-bit). It provides a clear, immersive soundstage for the tropical soundtrack and ensures dialogue is crisp even during busy comedic set-pieces.
