The Jungle is Alive... And It fights Back!

Welcome to the Hero Factory, where the most advanced Heroes in the universe are built to save the world from evil.

When rookie Rocka responds to a distress call from the planet Quatros, he discovers it is the planet itself that needs help! Rocka is taken hostage by the former Hero Professor Aldous Witch, who has now become the evil Witch Doctor.

The Alpha Team (Stormer, Furno, Nex, Stringer, and Bulk) must embark on a dangerous rescue mission. Equipped with new animal-based 3.0 powers, they must brave the savage jungle, battle possessed beasts, and stop the Witch Doctor from mining the planet’s Quaza core before Quatros is destroyed forever.
