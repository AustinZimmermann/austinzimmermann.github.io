LEGO Hero Factory: Savage Planet

DVD October 4, 2011 Warner Home Video Animated / Sci-Fi

Synopsis

A new Rookie, Rocka, does not listen to orders and heads to the planet Quatros to find the Witch Doctor. Legend says the planet is rich in Quaza, essential for its survival. When Rocka goes missing, the Alpha Team—Stormer, Furno, Nex, Stringer, and Bulk—must embark on a rescue mission. Upon arrival, they discover that the Witch Doctor is mining the Quaza core, destabilizing the planet. The Heroes must use their new 3.0 animal-based powers to navigate the savage jungle, battle possessed beasts, and stop the Witch Doctor before Quatros breaks apart!
