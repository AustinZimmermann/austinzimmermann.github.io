### Experience the Triumph in 1080p High Definition\!

From the producer of *Remember the Titans* and *Miracle* comes the inspirational true story of the team that changed college basketball forever.

Josh Lucas stars as Don Haskins, the charismatic college basketball coach who, in 1966, defied convention and fielded history’s first all-African American starting NCAA lineup. Against seemingly insurmountable odds, Texas Western College’s Miners united the nation and shocked the world with their thrilling journey to the national championship. Co-starring Jon Voight and Derek Luke, *Glory Road* is a breathtaking underdog story that shines brighter than ever on Blu-ray™ High Definition.
