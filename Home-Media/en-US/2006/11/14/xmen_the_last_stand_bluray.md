### **X-MEN: THE LAST STAND**

**The Tragic Finale of the X-Men Trilogy – Now on Blu-ray Disc\!**

In the climax of the *X-Men* motion picture trilogy, a “cure” for mutation threatens to alter the course of history. For the first time, mutants have a choice: retain their uniqueness, though it isolates and alienates them, or give up their powers to become human. The opposing viewpoints of mutant leaders Charles Xavier, who preaches tolerance, and Magneto, who believes in the survival of the fittest, are put to the ultimate test—triggering the war to end all wars.

  * **Street Date:** November 14, 2006
  * **MSRP:** $39.98
  * **Rating:** PG-13
  * **Runtime:** 104 Minutes

### **Blu-ray Exclusive Special Features**

This landmark release includes several high-definition exclusives designed to showcase the power of the Blu-ray format:

  * **DTS-HD Master Audio:** Experience bit-for-bit identical audio to the studio master.
  * **Smart Menu Technology:** Access the menu system without interrupting the film.
  * **High-Definition Trailers:** View the latest 20th Century Fox theatrical trailers in stunning 1080p.
