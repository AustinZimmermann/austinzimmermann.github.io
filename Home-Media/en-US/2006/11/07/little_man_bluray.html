Little Man

PG-13 2006 | 98 Minutes | Comedy

Synopsis

From the creators of White Chicks! After leaving prison, hard-boiled jewel thief Calvin Sims (Marlon Wayans) decides it’s time to retire, but not before one last big heist. A notorious crime boss offers Calvin and his partner Percy $100,000 to steal the famous Queen Diamond. But when the heist goes wrong, Calvin is forced to hide the diamond in a woman’s handbag. He soon discovers the woman and her husband are desperate for a child—so Calvin dresses up as a baby to get the diamond back!
