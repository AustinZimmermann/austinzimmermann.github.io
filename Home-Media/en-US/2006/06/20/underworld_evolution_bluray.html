Underworld: Evolution

“The saga continues...”

The saga of the war between the aristocratic Death Dealers (vampires) and the barbaric Lycans (werewolves) continues. The film traces the beginnings of the ancient feud between the two tribes as Selene, the beautiful vampire heroine, and Michael, the lycan hybrid, try to unlock the secrets of their lineages.

This modern-day tale of deadly action, ruthless intrigue, and forbidden love takes them into the battle to end all battles as the immortals must finally face their retribution.
