### **Synopsis**
Springing up from the turbulence of 9th Century China is the “House of the Flying Daggers,” a secret society of rebels fighting the corrupt government but gaining popular support by stealing from the rich to give to the poor. Two local captains, Leo (Andy Lau) and Jin (Takeshi Kaneshiro), are ordered to capture the mysterious new leader of the Flying Daggers. They suspect Mei (Ziyi Zhang), a beautiful blind showgirl, of being the leader’s daughter. Jin goes undercover to rescue her from prison and earn her trust, but on the perilous journey to the Daggers’ secret headquarters, the two fall hopelessly in love.

Directed by internationally acclaimed filmmaker Yimou Zhang (*Hero*).
