### **Product Overview**

The sci-fi masterpiece that launched a global phenomenon is now available in high-definition. Arnold Schwarzenegger stars as the most unrelenting, destructive machine ever to threaten the survival of mankind. An unstoppable cyborg from the future is sent back in time to eliminate Sarah Connor (Linda Hamilton), the woman whose unborn son will lead the human resistance against the machines.

Experience the film that redefined the action genre with the superior picture and sound quality of Blu-ray Disc.
