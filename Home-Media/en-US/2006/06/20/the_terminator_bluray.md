### **Synopsis**
In the Year of Darkness, 2029, the rulers of this planet are not men, but machines. Their destiny is to exterminate the human race. To alter the future, a cyborg assassin known as the Terminator (Arnold Schwarzenegger) is sent back in time to 1984 to kill Sarah Connor (Linda Hamilton), the woman whose unborn son will become the savior of humanity. The only thing standing in its way is Kyle Reese (Michael Biehn), a lone human soldier sent back from the future to protect her. 

Directed by James Cameron, this visionary sci-fi action thriller propelled Arnold Schwarzenegger to global superstardom and remains a pulse-pounding masterpiece.
