## Synopsis

**A New Breed of Secret Agent.**

Xander Cage (Vin Diesel) is a notorious underground thrill-seeker who, until now, has been deemed untouchable by the law. But when crack NSA Agent Gibbons (Samuel L. Jackson) convinces him to infiltrate a ruthless Russian crime ring, this new breed of secret agent (codename: xXx) takes down the enemies of justice with a vengeance in this high-octane, turbo-charged thrill-ride from the acclaimed director of *The Fast and the Furious*.

Starring Vin Diesel, Asia Argento, Marton Csokas, and Samuel L. Jackson. Directed by Rob Cohen.
