### Synopsis
A bachelor’s life is tough enough—try winning over the girl of your dreams every single day! Adam Sandler stars as Henry Roth, a marine life veterinarian working in Hawaii who finally meets the girl of his dreams, Lucy Whitmore (Drew Barrymore). There is just one catch: Lucy suffers from short-term memory loss and forgets everything the very next day. Because of her condition, Henry must invent new and imaginative ways to woo her anew each and every morning.
