Special Features

Alex “Hitch” Hitchens (Will Smith) is a legendary – and deliberately anonymous – New York City “date doctor” who, for a fee, has helped countless men woo the women of their dreams.

While coaching Albert (Kevin James), a meek accountant who is smitten with a glamorous celebrity, Allegra Cole (Amber Valletta), Hitch finally meets his match in the person of the gorgeous, bright-eyed Sara Melas (Eva Mendes), a gossip columnist who follows Allegra’s every move.

Now, on Blu-ray High Definition, experience the comedy that redefined the genre with crystal clear picture and uncompressed sound.
