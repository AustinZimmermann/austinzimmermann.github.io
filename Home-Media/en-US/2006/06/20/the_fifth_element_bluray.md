### **SYNOPSIS**
New York, the 23rd Century. The Earth is about to be destroyed by a huge ball of fire heading toward the planet. Cornelius, an old monk, knows how to stop the burning sphere: the Fifth Element, the Supreme Being, who unites the four basic Elements—air, water, fire, and earth—must be summoned, for it is the only weapon that can defeat the evil.

Bruce Willis, Milla Jovovich, and Gary Oldman star in director Luc Besson’s visually stunning sci-fi masterpiece. Now, experience the dazzling colors, explosive action, and immersive soundscapes of *The Fifth Element* in spectacular 1080p High Definition!
