### SYNOPSIS
The late 21st century. A subculture of humans has emerged, genetically modified by a vampire-like disease (Hemophagia) that enhances their speed, strength, and intelligence. As the human government sets out to destroy them, the infected are pushed to the brink of a global civil war. 

In the middle of the crossfire stands one woman: ultra-beautiful, ultra-lethal Violet (Milla Jovovich). With eye-popping martial arts skills and chameleon-like abilities, Violet becomes a rogue warrior bent on protecting her new race and seeking revenge on those who created it. When she finds herself protecting a mysterious 9-year-old boy (Cameron Bright) marked for death, Violet must fend off the human government and the infected alike. 

* **Starring:** Milla Jovovich, Cameron Bright, Nick Chinlund, and William Fichtner.
* **Directed by:** Kurt Wimmer
