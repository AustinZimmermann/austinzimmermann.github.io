THE PUNISHER

R 2004 124 Minutes June 27, 2006 (USA Release)

“This is not revenge. Revenge is not a valid motive, it’s an emotional response. No, not revenge... punishment.”

Frank Castle (Thomas Jane) is an FBI undercover agent who is looking to retire and settle down with his family. However, a final op results in the death of the son of crime lord Howard Saint (John Travolta), who retaliates by slaughtering Castle’s entire family. Armed with the tools of his trade, Castle becomes a one-man army: The Punisher.
