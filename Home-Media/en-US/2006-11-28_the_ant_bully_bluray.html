Synopsis

Julia Roberts, Nicolas Cage, Meryl Streep and Paul Giamatti head the voice cast of this animated adventure. New to the neighborhood, 10-year-old Lucas Nickle takes out his frustration on the innocent ants in his yard. But the ants use a magic potion to shrink Lucas down to their size! Now, he must survive in the colony, learning the ways of the insects and gaining a new perspective on life.
