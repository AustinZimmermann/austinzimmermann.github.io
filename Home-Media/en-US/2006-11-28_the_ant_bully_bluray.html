From Academy Award-nominated filmmaker John A. Davis (Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius) and producers Tom Hanks and Gary Goetzman comes a delightful adventure about a young boy who learns some very big lessons about life after he is shrunk down to the size of an ant.

Poor 10-year-old Lucas Nickle can’t get a break. Recently moved to a new city with his family, he hasn’t made a single friend. Taking out his frustration on the anthill in his front yard, Lucas is about to find out how it feels to be the little guy. A magical potion shrinks him down to insect size, and he is sentenced to hard labor in the ruins of the colony he destroyed.
