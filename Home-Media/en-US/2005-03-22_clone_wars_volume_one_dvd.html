Star Wars: Clone Wars

Volume One Lucasfilm Ltd. | Distributed by 20th Century Fox
MSRP $19.98
IN STOCK

The Saga continues with the Emmy® Award-winning animated series.

Witness the specific adventures that bridge the gap between Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones and Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. Produced by Lucasfilm Ltd. and directed by Genndy Tartakovsky (Samurai Jack), this animated series captures the Clone Wars action with a unique visual style.

Deep in the circle of the Clone Wars, the Jedi Knights struggle to maintain order. Yoda, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker, Mace Windu and other Jedi lead the clone army against the droid forces of the Separatists.

Special Features

  • Exclusive "Connecting the Saga" Featurette
  • Audio Commentary by Director Genndy Tartakovsky
  • Hyperspace: Inside Star Wars Featurette
  • The Making of Clone Wars
  • Galleries, Storyboards, and Concept Art
  • Episode III Revenge of the Sith Teaser Trailer
  • Playable Game Level: Star Wars: Republic Commando (Xbox Demo)
