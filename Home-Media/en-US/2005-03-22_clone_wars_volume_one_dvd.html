The Saga Continues...

Witness the animated micro-series that bridges the gap between Star Wars: Episode II - Attack of the Clones and Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith. Produced by Genndy Tartakovsky (Samurai Jack), this volume collects Chapters 1 through 20 of the acclaimed series.

As the Clone Wars sweep through the galaxy, the heroic Jedi Knights struggle to maintain order and restore peace. More and more systems are falling prey to the forces of the dark side as the Galactic Republic slips further and further under the sway of the Separatists and their never-ending droid army.

See Anakin Skywalker’s rise to knighthood, the introduction of the villainous Asajj Ventress, and the debut of General Grievous.

  • Audio Commentary: Commentary by Director Genndy Tartakovsky and artists.
  • “Bridging the Saga”: Featurette on the connection between Clone Wars and Revenge of the Sith.
  • Behind the Scenes: Making-of documentary.
  • Galleries: Sketches, storyboards, and posters.
  • Trailers: Star Wars: Episode III teaser trailer included.
  • Game Demo: Star Wars: Republic Commando Xbox demo level.
