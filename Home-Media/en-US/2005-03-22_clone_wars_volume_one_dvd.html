Begun, the Clone War Has

Witness the animated micro-series that bridges the saga between Attack of the Clones and Revenge of the Sith.

The Jedi Knights lead the Grand Army of the Republic against the droid forces of the Separatists. From the deserts of Muunilinst to the jungles of Yavin 4, Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi face new threats, including the dark acolyte Asajj Ventress and the bounty hunter Durge.
