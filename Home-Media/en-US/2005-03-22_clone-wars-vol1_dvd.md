## **Star Wars: Clone Wars – Volume One**

**“The Saga Continues in the Emmy®-Winning Animated Series!”**

### **Product Overview**

From director Genndy Tartakovsky comes the epic micro-series that bridges the gap between *Star Wars*: Episode II *Attack of the Clones* and the upcoming theatrical event *Star Wars*: Episode III *Revenge of the Sith* (In theaters May 19!).

**Release Date:** March 22, 2005

**Format:** DVD

**Price:** $19.98 MSRP

***

### **DVD Special Features**

* **Episodes 1–20:** All chapters from Seasons 1 and 2 seamlessly edited together.
* **“Bridging the Gap” Featurette:** Exclusive interviews and behind-the-scenes footage.
* **Director’s Commentary:** Insight from Genndy Tartakovsky and the production team.
* **Galleries:** Sketches, storyboards, and posters.
* **Video Game Trailer:** First look at *Star Wars*: Episode III *Revenge of the Sith* the video game.
* **Episode III Teaser:** An exclusive look at the final chapter of the prequel trilogy.
