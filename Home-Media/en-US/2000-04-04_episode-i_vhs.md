### **SYNOPSIS**

Stranded on the desert planet Tatooine after rescuing young Queen Amidala from the impending invasion of Naboo, Jedi apprentice Obi-Wan Kenobi and his Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn discover a nine-year-old slave named Anakin Skywalker. Young Anakin is unusually strong in the Force, and Qui-Gon believes the boy is the “Chosen One” who will bring balance to the Force.
