Welcome to Sky High, a high school that is the first and only of its kind. When you’re the son of the world’s most legendary superheroes, Commander (Kurt Russell) and Jetstream (Kelly Preston), there is only one school for you: Sky High, an elite institution trusted to mold today’s power-gifted students into tomorrow’s superheroes.

The problem is that Will Stronghold (Michael Angarano) is starting with absolutely no superpowers of his own. Worst of all, instead of joining the ranks of the “Hero” class, he finds himself relegated to being a “Sidekick”. Now he must somehow survive his freshman year while dealing with an overbearing gym coach, a bully who shoots fire from his hands, and a dangerous rebel with a grudge.
