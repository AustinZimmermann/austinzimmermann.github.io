Los Angeles SWAT cop Jack Traven (Keanu Reeves) is tasked with a terrifying hostage situation. A vengeful extortionist (Dennis Hopper) has rigged a city bus with a bomb that will explode if the bus drops below 50 miles per hour. With the help of a brave passenger (Sandra Bullock) who takes the wheel, Jack and his partner must figure out a way to save the passengers before time runs out.
