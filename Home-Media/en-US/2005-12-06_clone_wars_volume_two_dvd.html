Synopsis

The Emmy Award-winning animated series continues with Volume Two. Directed by Genndy Tartakovsky, this volume captures the clone wars’ most dramatic battles leading directly into the opening scene of Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith.

Witness Anakin Skywalker’s final trial to become a Jedi Knight, and see the terrifying power of General Grievous as he leads an assault on Coruscant to kidnap Chancellor Palpatine.

Description

From the creative mind of Genndy Tartakovsky (Samurai Jack, Dexter’s Laboratory) comes the second volume of the micro-series that bridged the gap between Attack of the Clones and Revenge of the Sith. This DVD collects Chapters 21 through 25. The animation style is distinct, kinetic, and stylized, offering a unique take on the Star Wars galaxy.
