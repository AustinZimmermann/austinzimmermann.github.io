SYNOPSIS

The Emmy® Award-winning animated series continues with Volume Two. Directed by Genndy Tartakovsky, this micro-series captures the battles, trials, and triumphs of the Jedi Knights and the Clone Army against the Separatist forces.

As the Clone Wars rage on, the Jedi Knights lead the clone army against the Separatists in the final chapter of the animated saga that leads directly into Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. Witness the trials of Anakin Skywalker and the secret ceremony that transforms him into a Jedi Knight.
