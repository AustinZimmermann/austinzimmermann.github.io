### **Product Overview**

When you’re as small as an ant, the world is a big place! From Academy Award®-nominated producer Tom Hanks and director John A. Davis comes a giant-sized comedy adventure.

After Lucas Nickle floods an ant colony with his garden hose, the ants use a magic potion to shrink him down to their size. To regain his freedom, Lucas must learn to live among the colony and discover the true meaning of friendship and courage.
