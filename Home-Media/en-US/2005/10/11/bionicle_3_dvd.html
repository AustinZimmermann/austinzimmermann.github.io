Web of Shadows

The Web of Shadows is Spinning...

Great Rescue, Great Trap! The Toa Metru return to Metru Nui to rescue the slumbering Matoran, only to find their beautiful home shrouded in toxic webs by the ruthless King Sidorak and Viceroy Roodaka. Mutated into monstrous beast-like Toa Hordika, they must learn to master their new primal natures and team up with the mysterious Rahaga before the Visorak swarms consume them forever!
