Performance Guide & SSML

🌟 1. Performance Emotion Brackets

Gemini 2.5 TTS models feature direct emotional steering via natural bracket commands. Prepend any word or sentence with these instructions:

  • [χαρούμενα] - Cheerful pitch, fast pace
  • [ψιθυριστά] - Intimate, whispery quality
  • [σοβαρά] - Direct, steady, educational
  • [λυπημένα] - Melancholic inflection
  • [θυμωμένα] - Sharp, passionate accents
  • [ενθουσιασμένα] - High-energy, positive inflection

⏳ 2. Structural Speech Pauses

Inserting breaks lets you craft highly polished speeches. Combine these standard markers to space words appropriately:

<break time="1s"/> // 1-second silence

<break time="500ms"/> // half-second break

📢 3. Pitch & Tone (Prosody)

Use wrapping tags to control the precise acoustic delivery of Greek pronunciation:

<prosody pitch="+15%">Υψηλός τόνος</prosody>

<prosody pitch="-10%">Χαμηλός τόνος</prosody>

<emphasis level="strong">Έμφαση</emphasis>

🗣️ 4. Dual-Voice Dialouges

Toggle dialogue mode to enable interaction. Simply assign character lines to trigger automatic multi-speaker configurations:

Σωκράτης: Γεια σου Γλάυκων.

Γλαύκων: Χαίρε, Σωκράτη!
