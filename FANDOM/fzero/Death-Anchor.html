Death Anchor

The Death Anchor (デスアンカー, Desu Ankā) is a high-performance racing machine in the F-Zero series. Piloted by the notorious villain Zoda, it is easily recognized by its dark purple chassis and the menacing skull emblem featured prominently on its nose.

Description

The Death Anchor was originally a missile stolen from the Galactic Federation. Zoda’s technicians modified the propulsion systems and added a cockpit to convert it into a racing craft. Because of its military origins, the machine possesses incredible acceleration and top speed, but suffers from a notoriously fragile hull.

In F-Zero GX, the Death Anchor is considered a “top-tier” machine for expert players. Its Boost Rank A allows it to sustain incredible speeds, but its Body Rank E means a single collision at high velocity can lead to a machine explosion.

Pilot

Zoda

Zoda is the leader of a cult of followers who aim to take over the universe. He is the arch-nemesis of Captain Falcon and Super Arrow.

History

  • F-Zero X: Debuted as Zoda’s signature craft. Notable for its high speed and difficult handling.
  • F-Zero GX: Featured with improved visual details, including glowing thrusters and a more defined skull motif.
  • F-Zero: GP Legend: Appears as a recurring antagonist machine in the anime-based series.
