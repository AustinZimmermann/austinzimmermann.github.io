The Death Anchor (デスアンカー, Desu Ankā) is a high-performance racing machine in the F-Zero series. It is the signature craft of the villainous Zoda and is widely regarded as one of the most dangerous machines on the circuit—both for its opponents and its pilot.

Design and Origin

Unlike most F-Zero machines which are built from the ground up for racing, the Death Anchor was originally an interplanetary ballistic missile. Stolen from the Galactic Space Federation by the secret society known as Deathriddle, it was extensively remodeled into a racing craft.

The machine retains many of its original military characteristics, including a sleek, missile-like aerodynamic frame and incredibly powerful propulsion systems. A little-known fact among fans is that the machine still contains an active self-destruct device inside, a detail that Zoda is reportedly unaware of.

Pilot: Zoda

Zoda

“The Incarnation of Evil”

Zoda is the leader of a cult-like organization and a long-time rival of Captain Falcon. Known for his manic personality and ruthless tactics, he uses the Death Anchor to spread terror across the F-Zero Grand Prix. In F-Zero: GP Legend, his backstory is expanded as a recurring antagonist.

Game Appearances

F-Zero X

Debuted with a weight of 1620 kg. It established the machine’s reputation for having a “paper-thin” body but a terrifyingly fast boost.

F-Zero GX

Gained three small fins in the back and a lighter blue color scheme. Considered an S-tier machine for experts due to its “Momentum Turbo Slide” (MTS) potential and high top speed.

F-Zero Climax

Introduces the Hyper Death Anchor, a version piloted by Hyper Zoda with a Body rank of C and even higher weight (1670 kg).

Handling Profile

  • Acceleration: Average. Requires effective boosting to reach top speeds quickly.
  • Cornering: Stiff. Harder to initiate turns than lighter machines, but maintains line well once committed.
  • Durability: Minimal. The E-Rank Body means it can only take a few hits from walls or opponents before exploding.
