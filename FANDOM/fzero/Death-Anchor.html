The Death Anchor (デスアンカー, Desu Ankā) is a high-speed racing machine in the F-Zero series. It is piloted by Zoda, the leader of a cult and a long-time criminal rival of Captain Falcon. Known for its extreme speed and terrifying volatility, the Death Anchor is a “glass cannon” in every sense of the word.

Created by the secret organization BS Group, the machine was designed specifically to outpace any Galactic Federation pursuit vessel. While it boasts some of the highest top speeds in the grand prix, its paper-thin armor makes it a dangerous gamble for any pilot.

History

The Death Anchor first appeared in F-Zero X and has been Zoda’s signature ride ever since. Its design features a sleek, needle-like nose and massive rear engines, reflecting Zoda’s “win at any cost” mentality. In the anime F-Zero: GP Legend, the machine is depicted as a bio-mechanical terror that can even operate autonomously under Zoda's influence.
