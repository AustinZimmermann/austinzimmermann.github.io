The Little Wyvern (Japanese: リトルワイバーン, Hepburn: Ritoru Waibān) is a high-speed racing machine in the F-Zero series, piloted by James McCloud. Manufactured by Space Dynamics, it is widely recognized for its striking resemblance to the Arwing starfighter from the Star Fox franchise.

Designed for speed and agility over durability, the Little Wyvern is a glass cannon. While it possesses one of the weakest bodies in the Grand Prix, its exceptional boost capability and reliable grip make it a top choice for technical racers who can avoid collisions.

Design

The Little Wyvern features a aerodynamic, multi-wing design painted in metallic grey and blue. It utilizes a single powerful engine and several stabilizer fins that allow it to maintain high speeds through corners. Its aesthetic is a direct homage to the Arwing, and its pilot, James McCloud, shares the name of Fox McCloud’s father, further cementing the cross-over reference.

Trivia

  • The machine is named after a Wyvern, a legendary winged creature with a dragon’s head.
  • In F-Zero GX, if you look closely at the pilot profile of James McCloud, his team name is listed as Galaxy Dog, a pun on the Star Fox team.
  • The engine sound of the Little Wyvern in F-Zero X is notably higher pitched than most other machines, mimicking the futuristic whine of an Arwing.
  • James McCloud claims he built the machine to support his family, specifically mentioning his young son.
