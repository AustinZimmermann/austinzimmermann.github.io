The Little Wyvern (Japanese: リトルワイバーン/span>, Hepburn: Ritoru Waibān) is a machine in the F-Zero series, primarily piloted by the trained dancer and wild child, James McCloud. First appearing in F-Zero X, it is designated as machine number 10.

Despite its name, the Little Wyvern is surprisingly heavy, but makes up for its weight with agile handling and a respectable boosting capability. It is known for its incredibly fragile body, requiring pilots to avoid heavy contact with rival racers and course barriers.

Characteristics

The Little Wyvern is engineered for agility and acceleration. The machine possesses an “E” rank in Body strength, which means it takes massive damage from impacts and attacks. However, its “B” rank in both Boost and Grip allows it to navigate sharp turns effectively and recover speed quickly. The machine tends to drift slightly at high speeds, a trait that skilled pilots can use to their advantage on corner-heavy tracks.

Game Appearances

F-Zero X

In its debut appearance, the Little Wyvern is a solid mid-tier machine. Its grip is reliable, but players must be extremely cautious during Death Races or heavily crowded Grand Prix events due to its poor durability.

F-Zero GX

In F-Zero GX, the Little Wyvern retains its original stats. It remains a highly maneuverable machine with a snappy boost. Customization parts based on the Little Wyvern (such as the Wyvern body part) are also available in the garage for players to construct their own custom machines.

Trivia

  • The name “Wyvern” refers to a legendary bipedal dragon, fitting James McCloud’s aggressive, animalistic nature and wild fashion sense.
  • Because of its E-ranked body, it is tied with a few other machines (like the Twin Noritta) for being one of the most fragile vehicles on the grid.
