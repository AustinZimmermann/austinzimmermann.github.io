Red Gazelle

The Red Gazelle (Japanese: レッドガゼル, Hepburn: Reddo Gazeru) is machine number 01 in the F-Zero series. It is piloted by the cyborg Mighty Gazelle. Known for its sleek design and aerodynamic build, it focuses heavily on top speed and boosting capabilities at the expense of defensive strength.

History

Built by Cyber Stick Inc., the Red Gazelle was originally designed prior to the horrific accident that nearly claimed Mighty Gazelle’s life. Following his transformation into a cyborg, he returned to the circuit with a newly modified version of the Red Gazelle. Because Mighty Gazelle’s mechanical body is immune to standard physical trauma, the machine’s safety features were entirely stripped out to reduce weight and maximize speed.

Performance

The Red Gazelle is infamous among F-Zero fans as a “glass cannon”. It possesses one of the strongest and longest-lasting Boosts in the game, allowing it to recover from mistakes quickly or dominate straights. However, its Body rating is an ‘E’, making it incredibly susceptible to attacks from aggressive machines like the Wild Goose or Black Bull.

  • Pros: Excellent boost speed, quick acceleration, highly responsive handling.
  • Cons: Extremely fragile, low weight makes it easy to knock off course.

Appearances

The Red Gazelle has appeared in the following titles:

  • F-Zero X (Nintendo 64)
  • F-Zero GX (GameCube)
  • F-Zero: GP Legend (Game Boy Advance)
  • F-Zero Climax (Game Boy Advance)

Trivia

If the player completes a Grand Prix on Master difficulty with the Red Gazelle in F-Zero GX, they are treated to an ending movie where Mighty Gazelle transforms the machine into a robotic suit.
