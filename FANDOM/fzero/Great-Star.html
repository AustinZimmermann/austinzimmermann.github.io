Great Star

The Great Star (Japanese: グレートスター, Hepburn: Gurēto Sutā) is a racing machine in the F-Zero series, piloted by Mr. EAD. It first appeared in F-Zero X and has been a staple of the franchise ever since.

Designed by the mysterious Shiggs Mapone (a nod to Shigeru Miyamoto), the Great Star is known for its extreme boost capabilities and unique, sleigh-like aesthetic. It is widely considered a “technical” machine, requiring high skill to manage its poor grip and fragile body.

Design & Performance

The Great Star is a purple, hulking craft with a distinctive yellow star logo. Despite its massive weight (1870kg), it possesses one of the weakest frames in the Grand Prix. Its primary strategy involves utilizing its A-rank Boost to maintain high speeds, often relying on advanced techniques like “Momentum Throttle” to stay competitive.

Trivia

  • The name “Shiggs Mapone” is an anagrammatic reference to Shigeru Miyamoto.
  • Mr. EAD mentions in interviews that the machine was built using his “brother’s recycled parts”.
  • Despite its weight, it has an E-rank Body, making it a “glass cannon” on the track.
