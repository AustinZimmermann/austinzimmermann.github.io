Iron Tiger

The Iron Tiger (Japanese: アイアンタイガー, Hepburn: Aian Taigā) is an F-Zero machine belonging to the pilot Baba. It made its debut in F-Zero X and has appeared in multiple titles across the franchise. Known for its distinctive yellow-and-black striped paint job, the machine is highly favored by racers who prefer tight handling and high grip over straight-line boosting capabilities.

Characteristics

Designed for complex, twisty circuits, the Iron Tiger boasts an exceptional ‘A’ rating in Grip. However, it sacrifices boost power, making it challenging to use on circuits with long straightaways like Mute City. The machine’s sturdy ‘B’ class body provides a good amount of durability against aggressive competitors.

Trivia

  • The Iron Tiger’s design heavily mirrors Baba’s own fashion, acting as an extension of his flashy, animal-print aesthetics.
  • In F-Zero: GP Legend, the Iron Tiger’s stats are slightly altered to accommodate the 2D gameplay style.
  • Despite its low boost rating, advanced players often use the Iron Tiger’s high grip to execute perfect drift turns, maintaining speed through corners where other machines would slow down.
