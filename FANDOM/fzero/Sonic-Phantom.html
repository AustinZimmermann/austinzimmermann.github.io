Sonic Phantom

The Sonic Phantom (ソニックファントム, Sonikku Fantomu) is an F-Zero racing Machine made specifically for The Skull.

Description

The Sonic Phantom was developed by Abinus, an entertainment company known for mixing black magic and science (and possibly responsible for The Skull’s return to life). The machine is truly unique among the Grand Prix for being the first to use Aura Engines that operate off the reactive power of living organisms.

However, there is a rumor lurking that The Skull uses black magic to suck the energy for his engines from the race spectators. It is the only machine with no steering device. Instead, The Skull uses said black magic to steer his craft.

The Sonic Phantom’s appearance appears to be based off of a long type of Drag Racing car. This type of vehicle perfectly suits its pilot's dangerous need for speed.

Official data

F-Zero GX Complete Guidebook (translated)

“This machine is made by Anubis, a company that considers entertainment through the power of science and black magic. It is equipped with a compact aura engine that utilizes bio-reactive energy for the first time in history. The boost performance of this lightweight machine is extremely excellent. However, there is a frightening rumor that the machine's energy source is sucked from F-ZERO spectators by the power of black magic.”

Trivia

  • The company that built the Sonic Phantom, Abinus Company, could be a reference to Anubis, the Egyptian god of the dead. It is unclear if the English spelling is a mistranslation from the original Japanese text.
  • Its Engine type (CY) is shared with the Red Gazelle, Mad Wolf, and Groovy Taxi.
  • The white lines on the machine resemble ribs, referencing the fact that The Skull is an undead skeleton.
  • In F-Zero GX, despite having no steering wheel, The Skull is still animated as if he is holding one.
