The Golden Fox (Japanese: ゴールデンフォックス, Hepburn: Gōruden Fokkusu) is an F-Zero machine driven by Dr. Stewart. It has appeared in every game in the F-Zero franchise since the original SNES title.

Known for its bright yellow chassis, the Golden Fox is highly specialized. It boasts incredible acceleration and one of the strongest boost speeds in the circuit, allowing Dr. Stewart to quickly recover from crashes or overtake opponents on straightaways. However, it suffers from poor grip and a fragile body, making it difficult for beginners to handle on tracks with sharp corners or aggressive opponents.

Characteristics

The Golden Fox was originally built by Dr. Stewart’s father, Professor Kevin Stewart. After his passing, Dr. Stewart modified the machine to honor his legacy. The machine relies heavily on its top-tier thrusters. When Boosting, the Golden Fox accelerates at a phenomenal rate, effectively making it a bullet on the track.

  • Strengths: Phenomenal acceleration, outstanding boost speed and duration, fast recovery time.
  • Weaknesses: Weak body, slippery handling, easily bullied by heavier machines like the Wild Goose or Black Bull.

Trivia

  • The Golden Fox is one of the original four machines introduced in F-Zero (SNES), alongside the Blue Falcon, Wild Goose, and Fire Stingray.
  • In F-Zero GX, the Golden Fox’s garage theme reflects Dr. Stewart’s sophisticated, playboy persona.
  • Because of its low weight and high boost, it is a highly favored machine in Time Attack modes across multiple games.
