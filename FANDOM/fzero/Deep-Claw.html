The Deep Claw (Japanese: ディープクロー, Hepburn: Dīpu Kurō) is an F-Zero machine piloted by the Takorian racer, Octoman. It made its first appearance in F-Zero X and has remained a staple in the series since. Identifiable by its distinct pinkish-red hue and tentacle-like design motifs, the Deep Claw is known for its balanced, if slightly slippery, handling.

Profile

Designed to replicate the agility of the Takorian species, the Deep Claw features aerodynamic fins and a robust dual-engine setup. Octoman entered the F-Zero Grand Prix hoping to use his winnings to pay off the debts of his home planet, Takora. The Deep Claw was heavily funded by an underground syndicate, though Octoman prefers to keep the exact nature of his sponsors a secret.

Characteristics

The Deep Claw boasts a solid B-rank in both Body and Boost. This makes it an incredibly sturdy contender in combat situations, capable of surviving aggressive side-attacks while delivering punishing blows to lighter machines. Its Boost is highly effective, allowing for quick recovery after taking corners.

However, its greatest weakness lies in its C-rank Grip. The machine has a tendency to slide heavily during sharp turns, requiring the pilot to master drift mechanics and blast-turning to maintain optimal racing lines.

History

F-Zero X

The Deep Claw debuted in F-Zero X as one of the starting machines. Due to the physics engine of the game, it was favored by intermediate players who wanted a machine that could survive the chaotic 30-racer scuffles while maintaining respectable speed.

F-Zero GX

In F-Zero GX, the Deep Claw’s sliding physics became more pronounced. It is highly favored by players utilizing the “snaking” technique (advanced maneuvering involving rapid left-and-right inputs) due to its specific acceleration curve and grip threshold.

Trivia

  • The Deep Claw’s design subtly resembles a squid or octopus, reflecting Octoman’s aquatic alien heritage.
  • In F-Zero GX, the Deep Claw has one of the loudest engine noises in the game, featuring a unique, bubbling aquatic hum.
  • Despite Octoman’s rivalry with Jody Summer, the Deep Claw’s stats are actually quite similar to her machine, the White Cat, though the Deep Claw is significantly heavier.
