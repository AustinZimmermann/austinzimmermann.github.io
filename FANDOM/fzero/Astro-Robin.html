The Astro Robin (アストロロビン, Asutoro Robin) is a racing machine in the F-Zero series, piloted by Jack Levin. It first appeared in F-Zero X and has been featured in every subsequent installment of the franchise.

Background

The Astro Robin was manufactured by Team Lightning Bolt, a renowned group within the F-Zero “old guard”. The machine was specifically designed as a high-tech, lightweight vehicle to complement Jack Levin’s “lightning” racing style. Because of Jack’s massive popularity as a former pop idol, the cockpit is famously equipped with numerous micro-cameras that broadcast his every move to his adoring female fan base during races.

Performance

Across the series, the Astro Robin is characterized by its exceptional Grip and Acceleration, though it often suffers from a below-average Boost capability. It is considered a very stable machine, making it a popular choice for beginners or players who prefer technical control over raw speed.

  • F-Zero X: Balanced machine with high grip, making it reliable on twisty tracks.
  • F-Zero GX: Features excellent acceleration even at maximum speed settings. While its top speed is lower than heavy hitters like the Black Bull, its handling is top-tier.
  • F-Zero: GP Legend: Known for being highly responsive with a strong turning radius.

Trivia

  • The name “Astro Robin” combines space themes (Astro) with bird motifs (Robin), reflected in the machine’s wing-like side panels.
  • In the anime F-Zero: GP Legend, the machine was briefly stolen by Lisa Brilliant.
  • Its number, 14, is a mainstay for Jack Levin.
