The Blue Falcon (Japanese: ブルーファルコン, Hepburn: Burū Farukon) is Captain Falcon’s signature racing machine in the F-Zero series. With the machine number 07, it has appeared in every single game in the franchise. It is arguably the most recognizable vehicle in the series.

The machine is highly balanced, making it an excellent choice for beginners to learn the mechanics of the game, while remaining a viable option for veterans.

Characteristics

The Blue Falcon is famous for its mid-tier performance ratings: B for Body, C for Boost, and B for Grip. This stat distribution ensures that the machine has no glaring weaknesses, although it lacks a specialized strength. It accelerates smoothly, maintains corners dependably, and can take a moderate amount of damage before exploding.

History

First debuting in the original F-Zero for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System, the Blue Falcon established the baseline for all subsequent machines. In F-Zero X and F-Zero GX, the engine was continually updated, keeping its weight at a steady 1260 kg.

Other Appearances

Beyond the F-Zero universe, the Blue Falcon has made numerous cameo appearances:

  • Super Smash Bros. series: Appears as Captain Falcon’s Final Smash, as well as an interactive stage element on tracks like Mute City and Port Town Aero Dive.
  • Mario Kart 8 / Deluxe: Featured as a playable kart body, continuing Nintendo’s tradition of cross-franchise cameos.

Trivia

In the anime F-Zero: GP Legend, the Blue Falcon is depicted as having advanced AI capabilities, allowing it to drive to Captain Falcon's location autonomously when summoned.
