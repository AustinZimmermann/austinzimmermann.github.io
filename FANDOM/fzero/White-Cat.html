White Cat

The White Cat (Japanese: ホワイトキャット, Hepburn: Howaito Kyatto) is a machine piloted by Jody Summer, the renowned combat pilot of the Galactic Space Federation. First introduced in F-Zero X, the machine bears the number 02.

Built exclusively for Jody Summer, the White Cat possesses phenomenal cornering ability and grip, allowing it to navigate tight, technical tracks with ease. While its body strength and boost power are generally average, its handling makes it a favorite for beginners and veterans alike.

Characteristics

The White Cat features a sleek, aerodynamic design heavily utilizing white and hot pink colors, resembling the feline creature it is named after. Due to Jody Summer’s affiliation with the Galactic Space Federation, the machine is equipped with state-of-the-art GSF technology to maximize grip.

Trivia

  • In the anime F-Zero: GP Legend, the White Cat is occasionally seen utilizing weapons, reflecting Jody’s role in the Mobile Task Force.
  • Despite having a ‘C’ in Body, its actual durability in F-Zero GX makes it quite resilient compared to other light-weight machines.
