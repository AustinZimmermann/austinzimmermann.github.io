## ⌨️ Simulator Action Log

* `Press [DISPLAY]` ➔ Toggles the on-screen bit-rate meter. Current video bitrate fluctuates aggressively between **18 Mbps** and **28 Mbps**.
* `Press [POP-UP MENU]` ➔ The dynamic menu slides up smoothly from the bottom of the screen without pausing the action at the Golden Gate Bridge scene.
* `Press [STOP]` ➔ Audio/video cuts out completely. Screen reads `⏹️ STOPPED`. *(Note: Due to early BD-Java code structures on this disc, pressing Play will restart the disc from the beginning instead of resuming where you left off).*
