## 🎬 Disc Information: Enemy of the State (1998)

* **Format:** BD-50 (Dual-Layer, 50GB) — *One of Disney’s earliest dual-layer releases!*
* **Video Codec:** MPEG-2 (1080p, 2.40:1 Aspect Ratio)
* **Main Audio:** Uncompressed Linear PCM 5.1 (48kHz/24-bit, 6.9 Mbps)
* **Secondary Audio:** Dolby Digital 5.1 (640 kbps)

## 🎮 Remote Control Input Simulator

*To send a command to the player, imagine pressing one of the physical remote buttons below:*

### **Playback Transport**

* **`[ PLAY ]`** ➔ Resumes the high-bitrate 1080p stream.
* **`[ PAUSE ]`** ➔ Freezes on a razor-sharp uncompressed frame.
* **`[ STOP ]`** ➔ *Warning:* This early player lacks resume-play for BD-Java discs. Pressing play again will restart the disc from the Disney “Blue Castle” intro.

### **Navigation & Menus**

* **`[ POP-UP MENU ]`** ➔ Triggers the futuristic blue BD-Java menu overlay seamlessly on top of the movie while it keeps playing.
* **`[ AUDIO ]`** ➔ Toggles between `Track 1: English LPCM 5.1` and `Track 2: English DD 5.1`. *(Noticeable audio drop for 2 seconds while the 2006 receiver re-clocks).*

## 🛠️ Hardware Setup Diagnostics (2006)

* **Video Connection:** HDMI 1.2a ➔ Outputting fixed **1080p/60Hz** *(Note: 24p true cinema output is not supported by this 2006 hardware; the player is executing a 3:2 pulldown).*
* **Audio Connection:** HDMI Multi-Channel Linear PCM ➔ Sent directly to an HDMI-capable A/V Receiver to handle the uncompressed audio track.
* **HDCP Status:** `HDCP 1.1 Handshake Successful`.
