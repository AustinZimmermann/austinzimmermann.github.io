## ⌨️ Simulator Action Log

* `Press [POP-UP MENU]` ➔ Blu-ray's Java-based profile allows the menu to slide over the live video without stopping Reed Richards' speech.
* `Press [AUDIO]` ➔ Swapping from the high-bitrate *DTS-HD MA* core track over to the *Dolby Digital 5.1* Spanish track.
* `Press [STOP]` ➔ Video feed drops. Front panel displays `■ STOPPED`.
> ⚠️ **Format Note (2006):** Unlike DVD, standard BD-J profiles do not support automatic "resume play" on this hardware layer yet. Pressing Play will restart the disc from the Fox logo screen.
