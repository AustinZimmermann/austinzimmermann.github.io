Synopsis

Based on the graphic novel by Frank Miller and Lynn Varley, 300 is a fictionalized retelling of the Battle of Thermopylae in 480 BC. It depicts King Leonidas (Gerard Butler) leading 300 Spartans against the Persian “god-King” Xerxes (Rodrigo Santoro) and his massive army. The Blu-ray release was one of the early high-definition titles, showcasing the film’s highly stylized, comic-book aesthetic with vibrant color grading and stark visual fidelity, making it a popular showcase disc for the emerging format.
