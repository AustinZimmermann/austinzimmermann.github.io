At a Glance

This section provides the primary release details for this 2006 Japanese Blu-ray. This was one of 20th Century Fox’s initial offerings for the new high-definition format, and its specifications reflect the technology available at the time.

Release Date November 10, 2006
Studio 20th Century Fox

    Japanese, English (Likely)

The initial Blu-ray releases of Fantastic Four were noted for containing significantly fewer special features than their standard-definition DVD counterparts. This was largely due to the technical limitations described in the next section.

Expected Features on this Release:

  • Deleted/Extended Scenes (A small selection)
  • Commentary Track (Often carried over from DVD)
  • Theatrical Trailers/Promos

The vast, in-depth featurettes and documentaries found on the DVD version were generally omitted from this first-generation Blu-ray.
