Poor Lucas Nickle. The new kid in town, he's got no friends and he's a prime target for the local bully. But Lucas has a way of letting off steam - he terrorizes the tiny, defenseless ants in his yard by flooding and stomping their hill.


But the ants have had enough. Using a magic potion, they shrink Lucas down to ant size and sentence him to live like an ant in their colony. In this strange new world, Lucas will learn the value of friendship and teamwork, gain a whole new perspective on life, and finally find the courage to stand up for himself.
