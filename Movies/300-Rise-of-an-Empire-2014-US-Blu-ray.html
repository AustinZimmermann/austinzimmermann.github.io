Film Summary

Based on Frank Miller’s graphic novel Xerxes, the film follows the Greek general Themistocles as he attempts to unite all of Greece and battle the massive invading Persian forces, led by Xerxes, the mortal-turned-god. The decisive confrontation takes place at sea, where Themistocles faces off against Artemisia, the ruthless commander of the Persian navy, in a bloody and stylized naval war that parallels the heroic stand at Thermopylae.

“The only hope for Greece is the unification of its people.”
