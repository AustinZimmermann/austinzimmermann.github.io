Synopsis

Frank Martin (Jason Statham) is a “transporter”—a mercenary driver who transports anything, anywhere, no questions asked. When he breaks his own rules and opens a package, he discovers a beautiful Chinese woman named Lai (Shu Qi) who is being trafficked. Frank is forced to confront the criminal organization responsible, leading to a high-octane chase across France filled with explosive action and martial arts.
