Star Wars: The Complete Saga (2011 US Blu-ray)

Release Date: September 16, 2011 (US)

Format: 9-Disc Blu-ray Set (6 Films + 3 Bonus Discs)

🎬 Film Discs (Discs 1-6)

Disc Title Content
Disc 1 Star Wars: Episode I – The Phantom Menace Feature Film + Original DVD Audio Commentary + New Archival Audio Commentary
Disc 2 Star Wars: Episode II – Attack of the Clones Feature Film + Original DVD Audio Commentary + New Archival Audio Commentary
Disc 3 Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith Feature Film + Original DVD Audio Commentary + New Archival Audio Commentary
Disc 4 Star Wars: Episode IV – A New Hope Feature Film + Original DVD Audio Commentary + New Archival Audio Commentary
Disc 5 Star Wars: Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back Feature Film + Original DVD Audio Commentary + New Archival Audio Commentary
Disc 6 Star Wars: Episode VI – Return of the Jedi Feature Film + Original DVD Audio Commentary + New Archival Audio Commentary

⭐ Bonus Content Discs (Discs 7-9)

Disc 7: Prequel Trilogy Bonus Features

  • Interviews (Cast and Crew)
  • Deleted/Extended Scenes (I-III)
  • “The Collection” (360 Turnarounds, Video Commentaries for Models/Maquettes/Costumes)
  • Concept Art Galleries
  • Archive Fly-Through (A look into the Lucasfilm Archives)

Disc 8: Original Trilogy Bonus Features

  • Interviews (Cast and Crew)
  • Deleted/Extended Scenes (IV-VI)
  • “The Collection” (360 Turnarounds, Video Commentaries for Models/Maquettes/Costumes)
  • Concept Art Galleries
  • Archive Fly-Through (A look into the Lucasfilm Archives)

Disc 9: Documentaries and Spoofs

  • “The Making of Star Wars” (1977 Documentary)
  • “The Empire Strikes Back: SPFX” (Documentary)
  • “Classic Creatures: Return of the Jedi” (Documentary)
  • “Anatomy of a Dewback” (Documentary)
  • “Star Warriors” (Documentary about the 501st Legion)
  • “Star Wars Tech” (Documentary)
  • “A Conversation with the Masters (ESB 30 Years Later)” (Documentary)
  • Star Wars Spoofs (Collection of parodies and references)
  • *Note: Disc 8 contains the “Star Wars Holiday Special” Cartoon Easter Egg (The Faithful Wookiee - Boba Fett’s first appearance).
