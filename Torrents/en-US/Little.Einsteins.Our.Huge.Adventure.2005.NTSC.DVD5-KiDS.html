Movie Overview

Get ready to blast off with the direct-to-DVD pilot film that launched the hit Playhouse Disney series, “Little Einsteins: Our Huge Adventure”! Created by Disney and the award-winning Baby Einstein Company, this full-length movie takes your child on an interactive, musical, and artistic journey around the globe.

Join Leo, June, Quincy, Annie, and Rocket as they go on their very first "mission" to help a tiny caterpillar find its musical tree of many colors to get a brand new outfit, and search the United States to find a butterfly’s lost invitation to a migration party in Angangueo, Mexico.

Featuring a revolutionary blend of 2D/3D animation, real-world live-action footage, historical works of art (such as Van Gogh’s Wheat Field with Cypresses and Hokusai’s The Great Wave off Kanagawa), and beautiful classical music orchestration (principally featuring Beethoven’s Ninth Symphony).
