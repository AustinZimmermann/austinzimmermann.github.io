What is a Blu-ray Remux?

A Remux is a perfect bit-for-bit extraction of the video and audio streams from the commercial retail Blu-ray disc. Unlike a traditional transcode (like encode / x264), no video details are lost or compressed. It is wrapped in a modern MKV container, compatible with major home theater applications like Plex, Kodi, Infuse, and local media players.
