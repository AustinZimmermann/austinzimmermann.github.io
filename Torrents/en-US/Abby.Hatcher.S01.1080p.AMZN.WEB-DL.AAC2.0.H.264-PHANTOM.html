Synopsis

Abby Hatcher (also known as Abby Hatcher, Fuzzly Catcher) is an animated preschool series created by Rob Hoegee and produced by Guru Studio in conjunction with Spin Master Entertainment. The series follows a bright and energetic seven-year-old girl named Abby Hatcher and her Fuzzly friends, who are amazing creatures that live in her family’s hotel.

Abby and her best friend Bozzly go on adventures to help the Fuzzlies with their problems and fix any mishaps they cause, often using Abby’s high-tech gadgets like her Fuzzly Spotter and super-powered sneakers.
