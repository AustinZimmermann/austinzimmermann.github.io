“Musical Scales / Hand in Hand” is the third episode of the third season of the American-Canadian animated children’s television series Dragon Tales. It first aired on PBS Kids on February 23, 2005.

Plot

Musical Scales

Zak is feeling nervous about performing in a music festival. He discovers that his scales actually produce musical notes when touched. With the help of Emmy and Max, Zak learns to embrace his unique talent and gains the confidence to perform on stage with his friends.

Hand in Hand

Ord and Cassie find themselves stuck together after coming into contact with a “Sticky-Yicky Plant”. The episode follows their journey as they navigate Dragon Land while literally attached at the hip, learning that cooperation and communication are essential to solving their problem.

Educational Goals

Like most episodes of Dragon Tales, this episode focuses on emotional intelligence and social skills:

  • Self-Confidence: Overcoming stage fright and valuing one’s unique traits.
  • Cooperation: Working together to achieve a common goal when faced with a physical or metaphorical obstacle.
