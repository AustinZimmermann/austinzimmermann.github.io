“To Fly With a New Friend” is the first episode of the third season of the animated children’s television series Dragon Tales. It originally aired on PBS Kids on February 21, 2005. The episode is notable for introducing Enrique, a new human character who moves in next door to Emmy and Max, and becomes the third child to travel to Dragon Land.

Plot [edit]

The story begins with Emmy and Max noticing a new neighbor, Enrique, moving in next door. Enrique has recently moved from Colombia and is feeling homesick and anxious about his new environment. Emmy and Max decide to invite him to play, but Enrique is initially hesitant.

Hoping to cheer him up, Emmy and Max decide it’s time to share their secret. They invite Enrique to their playroom and recite the dragon scale chant. Enrique is astonished as they are transported to Dragon Land. Upon arrival, Enrique is overwhelmed by the fantastical sight of talking dragons and floating islands.

They meet up with Ord, Cassie, Zak, and Wheezie. The dragons are excited to meet a new friend, but Enrique’s fear of heights (acrophobia) becomes apparent when the group prepares to go for a flight. The episode focuses on the group helping Enrique overcome his fear and feel welcomed in his new “world,” paralleling his real-life move to a new country. By the end of the episode, Enrique learns that while things may be different and scary at first, they can also be wonderful.

Characters [edit]

  • Emmy: The adventurous older sister who takes charge.
  • Max: Emmy’s energetic younger brother.
  • Enrique: The new neighbor who is shy and speaks both English and Spanish.
  • Ord: A large, blue dragon who is often scared but very brave for his friends.
  • Cassie: A small, pink dragon who is shy and sweet.
  • Zak and Wheezie: A two-headed dragon with contrasting personalities.
  • Quetzal: The elderly dragon teacher who provides wisdom to the children.

Production and Themes [edit]

The introduction of Enrique in Season 3 was a strategic move by the producers to introduce more diversity and explore themes of immigration and cultural adjustment. Enrique frequently uses Spanish phrases, which served an educational purpose for the show’s audience.

The episode was written by the long-time writing duo Cliff Ruby and Elana Lesser, who were known for their work on various animated features. The animation style remained consistent with previous seasons, though some fans noted slight refinements in the character models for the third season.

Reception [edit]

“To Fly With a New Friend” was well-received by critics for its sensitive handling of homesickness and the anxieties associated with moving. The addition of Enrique was praised for bringing a fresh dynamic to the established trio of Emmy, Max, and the dragons.
