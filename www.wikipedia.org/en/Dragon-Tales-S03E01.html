“To Fly with a New Friend” is the first episode of the third season of the animated children’s television series Dragon Tales, and the 65th episode overall. This episode is notable for introducing Enrique, a new human character who moves in next door to Max and Emmy.

Plot

The story begins with Max and Emmy discovering that a new family has moved in next door. They meet Enrique, a boy who recently moved from Colombia. When Max and Emmy decide to visit Dragon Land, they are faced with a dilemma: whether or not to include Enrique.

Upon arriving in Dragon Land, Enrique is initially frightened by the dragons (Ord, Cassie, Zak, and Wheezie). The episode focuses on themes of inclusion, overcoming the fear of the unknown, and making new friends. Enrique eventually learns the dragon scale chant and begins to adjust to his new magical surroundings.

Production

This episode marked a significant soft reboot for the series. Along with the introduction of Enrique, the season featured updated animation techniques and a stronger emphasis on bilingual education, with Enrique often speaking Spanish phrases.

Reception

Critics noted that the addition of Enrique helped revitalize the series’ educational goals regarding cultural diversity. The episode is often cited by fans as a turning point in the show’s later years.
