X-MEN

Evolution Begins.

Movie
2000 • 1h 44m • Science Fiction, Action, Adventure • PG-13

In a world where mutants (humans with a genetic variation that grants them superpowers) are shunned and feared, two groups come to blows. One, led by the powerful telepath Professor Charles Xavier, seeks peaceful coexistence with humanity. The other, led by the master of magnetism Magneto, believes a war is coming and intends to strike first.
