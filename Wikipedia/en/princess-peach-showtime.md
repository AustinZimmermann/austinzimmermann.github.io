# *Princess Peach: Showtime!*

***Princess Peach: Showtime!*** (Japanese: プリンセスピーチ Showtime!, Hepburn: *Purinsesu Pīchi Shōtaimu!*) is an action-adventure video game developed by Good-Feel and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. Released globally on March 22, 2024, the game marks the first time Princess Peach has been the main protagonist of a video game since *Super Princess Peach* (2005) for the Nintendo DS.

## Gameplay

*Princess Peach: Showtime!* is a 3D action-adventure game set within the confines of a magical theater. The game is divided into various stages, each representing a different theatrical play. Players control Princess Peach as she navigates these stages, solving puzzles, defeating enemies, and rescuing kidnapped performers.

Peach is accompanied by **Stella**, the guardian of the theater, who grants Peach a magical ribbon. This ribbon allows Peach to interact with the environment, rally the theater’s cast (the Theets), and utilize the game’s core mechanic: **Transformations**.

On specific stages, Peach steps onto a transformation platform, taking on a starring role that grants her unique abilities tailored to the theme of the play.

### Notable Transformations

* **Swordfighter Peach:** Wields a rapier to gracefully parry, dodge, and strike enemies.
* **Detective Peach:** Investigates crime scenes, questions witnesses, and uses a magnifying glass to uncover hidden clues.
* **Patissier Peach:** Bakes and decorates desserts rapidly to appease hungry characters and solve culinary puzzles.
* **Kung Fu Peach:** Uses martial arts to launch foes, maneuver across poles, and break obstacles.
* **Ninja Peach:** Uses stealth, wall-running, and kunai to silently navigate enemy-infested environments.
* **Cowgirl Peach:** Rides a trusty horse and wields a lasso to grab enemies and items from afar.
* **Figure Skater Peach:** Glides gracefully across ice, performing spins and jumps to defeat enemies and light up the stage.
* **Dashing Thief Peach:** Uses grappling hooks and stealth to bypass security and steal back stolen goods.
* **Mermaid Peach:** Uses her voice to command schools of fish to solve underwater puzzles and open pathways.
* **Mighty Peach:** Dons a superhero suit to fly, lift heavy objects, and punch meteors out of the sky.

## Plot

Princess Peach, accompanied by several Toads, receives an invitation to attend a performance at the illustrious **Sparkle Theater**. Shortly after their arrival, a wicked sorceress named **Grape** and her henchmen, the **Sour Bunch**, attack the theater. They trap the guests inside, steal the theater’s Sparkle Gems, and take control of the various plays.

Peach partners with **Stella**, the star guardian of the theater, to take back the stage. By traversing the theater’s different floors, Peach must enter the corrupted plays, assume the lead roles, defeat Grape’s bosses, and restore the Sparkle Gems to return the theater to its former glory.

## Development

The game was first teased during a Nintendo Direct presentation in June 2023, where brief footage of Peach traversing a theatrical stage was shown. The game’s official title, *Princess Peach: Showtime!*, and its March 2024 release date were officially revealed in a subsequent Nintendo Direct in September 2023.

While Nintendo initially kept the developer a secret, dataminers and later the game’s credits confirmed that it was developed by **Good-Feel**, a studio known for their work on visually distinct Nintendo titles like *Kirby’s Epic Yarn* and *Yoshi’s Crafted World*. The game was built using Unreal Engine 4.

***

## Reception

*Princess Peach: Showtime!* received generally favorable reviews from critics upon release. Reviewers praised the game’s charming presentation, the creative variety of the transformations, and the detailed, diorama-like stage designs. The game was widely celebrated as a highly accessible, family-friendly entry in Nintendo’s library.

However, the game also faced some criticism. Many reviewers noted its low level of difficulty and relatively short completion time, suggesting it leaned heavily toward a younger demographic. Additionally, the game received some technical criticism for inconsistent framerates during busier gameplay moments.

Despite the critiques, the game was a commercial success, selling over 1.2 million copies within its first two weeks of release.
