Abby Hatcher is the main protagonist of the Canadian-American animated children’s television series of the same name. She is an intelligent, energetic, and kind seven-year-old girl who lives with her family in a magnificent hotel. Her primary role is that of a “Fuzzly Catcher” and “Fuzzly Friend”, dedicated to helping and protecting the magical, quirky creatures known as Fuzzlies that secretly reside in her family’s hotel.

Character Profile

Abby is depicted as resourceful and highly capable of problem-solving. She is notably one-half Chinese on her mother’s side. Her proactive approach to challenges and her deep empathy for the Fuzzlies form the core of the series’ action. She wears large glasses and typically sports a black shirt, pink skirt, and blue shoes.

Equipment and Abilities

Abby Hatcher utilizes several high-tech gadgets to assist her on her missions, which are typically initiated when a Fuzzly is in trouble.

  • Fuzzly Spotter: A special wrist device that detects when a Fuzzly needs help. It also possesses various functions for operating equipment within the hotel.
  • Spring Shoes: Boots equipped with springs that allow Abby to jump extraordinary heights.
  • Inflatable Coat: A coat that can inflate, granting Abby the ability to glide or fly short distances.
  • Trike: A specialized vehicle Abby uses for fast travel around the hotel and its grounds.

Relationship with Bozzly

Abby’s closest companion and co-adventurer is Bozzly, a rabbit-like Fuzzly with aquamarine fur. Bozzly is her best friend and partner in solving Fuzzly mishaps. His unique abilities, such as turning invisible and flying by spinning his ears like a propeller, often complement Abby’s high-tech equipment. Bozzly also has a mysterious front pocket that can hold objects much larger than himself, providing comic relief and handy tools.

The Fuzzlies

Abby is the only human known to be actively involved in helping the Fuzzlies, a group of colorful, unique, and often misunderstood creatures living in her hotel. Some of the notable Fuzzlies she regularly interacts with include:

  • Princess Flug: A pink, slug-like Fuzzly made of glitter goo who can blow neutralizing bubbles.
  • Teeny Terry: A small, round, marigold cat-bird-like Fuzzly who can inflate like a balloon to fly higher.
  • Otis: A red, raspberry-like Fuzzly with three extendable, sticky tentacles on his head.
  • Harriet Bouffant: A ragdoll-like Fuzzly whose signature feature is her extendable pink and yellow hair that functions as extra limbs.
  • The Squeaky Peepers: An octet of small, singing, rainbow-colored, rabbit-like Fuzzlies.
