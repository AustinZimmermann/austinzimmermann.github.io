“The Peeper Transport Tubes / Abby Loses Her Glasses” is the premiere episode of the second season of the Canadian animated children’s television series Abby Hatcher. It originally aired in the United States on Nick Jr. on March 8, 2020, and in Canada on April 20, 2020. The episode is split into two distinct 11-minute segments.

Contents

Plot

“The Peeper Transport Tubes”

When deliveries at the Hatcher Palace Hotel become overwhelmingly busy, Abby, her father, and Teeny Terry try to speed up the process by creating a transport tube system to deliver fresh blueberries to Chef Jeff. However, the system proves to be too chaotic. To improve it, Abby enlists the help of the Squeaky Peepers, creating the “Peeper Transport Tubes”. The system works perfectly at first, but when the hotel guests begin ordering too many items at once, the Peepers get confused, causing a mix-up in deliveries and eventually getting trapped at an intersection.

“Abby Loses Her Glasses”

During a game of soccer with Bozzly, Abby attempts to catch the ball but gets hit in the face, causing her glasses to fly off. Severely struggling to see without them, Abby has to rely on Bozzly and Princess Flug to guide her. The glasses are taken by various animals, including a squirrel, a frog, and a bird, leading the trio on a wild chase across the hotel grounds. Using her Fuzzly Spotter’s “glasses finder” feature, Abby tracks the beeps to retrieve her glasses, even managing to catch them mid-air while practically blind.

Trivia

  • This episode serves as the Season 2 premiere. It introduces a new version of the show’s intro sequence with remixed lyrics and the addition of the character Grumbles.
  • In “Abby Loses Her Glasses”, this marks the longest period in the series where Abby is seen without her glasses, severely impacting her ability to suit up and navigate.
  • This is the first time Bozzly is seen driving Abby’s bike.
  • These segments were among the final episodes premiered before the COVID-19 pandemic began.
