“Grumbles Gets Gone / Here an Abby, There an Abby” is the 26th episode of the first season of the Canadian-American animated children’s television series Abby Hatcher. It serves as the season finale for Season 1. The episode first aired on Nick Jr. in the United States on February 16, 2020.

The episode consists of two 11-minute segments. In the first segment, “Grumbles Gets Gone”, the Fuzzly character Grumbles is accidentally mistaken for a piece of furniture and taken away from the hotel. In the second segment, “Here an Abby, There an Abby”, Abby Hatcher attempts to solve multiple problems at the Hatcher Palace Hotel simultaneously, leading to confusion and chaos.

Plot

Grumbles Gets Gone

While playing hide-and-seek or relaxing in the hotel, Grumbles, a large purple Fuzzly who can transform into anything, morphs into a couch (or a similar piece of furniture) to hide. However, his transformation is so convincing that he is mistaken for an actual item to be removed or moved during a cleaning or renovation effort at the Hatcher Palace Hotel.

Grumbles is accidentally taken away from the hotel, loaded onto a vehicle, and transported to an unknown location. Upon realizing Grumbles is missing, Abby and Bozzly launch a rescue mission. They track the vehicle using Abby’s Fuzzly Spotter and eventually locate Grumbles. The segment focuses on Grumbles’ anxiety about being away from home and the Fuzzly Catchers’ determination to return him safely before he transforms back and reveals his true nature to humans who are unaware of Fuzzlies.

Here an Abby, There an Abby

It is a busy day at the Hatcher Palace Hotel, and everyone seems to need Abby's help at the exact same time. Princess Flug needs assistance with a glitter goo project, Chef Jeff is having trouble in the kitchen, and the Squeaky Peepers are causing their usual mischief.

Instead of prioritizing tasks or asking for help, Abby tries to be in multiple places at once, rushing frantically between floors and problems. In her haste, she mixes up the solutions—giving kitchen supplies to Princess Flug and glitter to Chef Jeff—causing the problems to escalate into disasters. Realizing that rushing has made things worse, Abby stops to breathe and comes up with a plan to organize the Fuzzlies to help each other, restoring order to the hotel.

Production

The episode was directed by Kyran Kelly, who served as a primary director for the first season of the series. The segment “Here an Abby, There an Abby” was written by Geri Cole.[1] The episode features the voice talents of Macy Drouin as Abby, Wyatt White as Bozzly, and Kaden Stephen as Grumbles.

Broadcast

“Grumbles Gets Gone / Here an Abby, There an Abby” premiered in the United States on the Nick Jr. Channel on February 16, 2020.[2] It aired as the finale of the first season, with the second season premiering shortly thereafter in March 2020.
